Suggest A Game or Simulation

We're constantly on the lookout for new agile games and activities that would benefit from the Agile Simulations treatment. The kind of thing we are looking for are those activities that you wouold normally do round a table; say in a retro or as a warm up activity.

We want to bring these activities to remote teams so they can intreract in a way that is as close to being round the same table as possible.

If you're wondering if your idea is suitable, ask these questions:

  • Does it involve lego, coins, playing cards or some other physical object that gets passed between participants?
  • Does it demonstrate somethiong about agile, lean, teams, etc.?
  • Is it really frustrating, or impossible to play it over Zoom or Teams?

If so, it's probably of interest...
